Terms & Conditions for Personal Loans

1. Loan Amount

Loan amounts provided range from ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 per individual. The exact loan amount will be agreed upon between the lender and borrower before disbursement.

2. Purpose of Loan

Loans are intended for personal use only, including educational expenses, emergency needs, or small business support. Loans cannot be used for illegal activities or speculative investments.

3. Repayment Terms

  • Borrowers must repay the loan according to the agreed repayment schedule.
  • Repayment can be made in full or installments, as mutually agreed.
  • Repayment will be made via bank transfer, UPI, or other agreed digital methods.

4. Interest

Interest, if applicable, will be charged at a mutually agreed rate per month, within legal limits set by the State Money Lenders Act. The interest rate and total repayment amount will be clearly documented in the loan agreement.

5. Default

  • If the borrower fails to repay the loan as per the schedule, the lender may:
    • Send reminders or notices requesting repayment.
    • Initiate legal action under the Civil Procedure Code (CPC) or applicable laws.
  • Borrowers are responsible for any additional legal costs incurred due to default.

6. Documentation

  • Borrowers must provide valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport) before loan disbursement.
  • Optional: Signature of a parent, guardian, or witness for students or minors.
  • All loan details will be recorded in a loan register for transparency.

7. Prepayment

Borrowers may repay the loan before the due date without penalty, unless otherwise specified.

8. Privacy

Borrower information and loan details will be kept confidential and not shared with third parties without consent.

9. Governing Law

This loan agreement will be governed by the laws of India, including the State Money Lenders Act applicable to the lender’s state. Any disputes will be addressed in the local civil court jurisdiction where the loan was disbursed.

10. Acceptance

By accepting the loan, the borrower acknowledges and agrees to all terms and conditions mentioned above.
